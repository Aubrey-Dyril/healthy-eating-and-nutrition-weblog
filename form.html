Healthy Eating

Eating healthy is about making smarter choices with the food we consume. It's about fueling our bodies with the right nutrients. A healthy diet helps maintain energy, promotes good health, and supports your body's natural systems.

Here are some tips to improve your eating habits:

  • Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables every day.
  • Choose whole grains like brown rice, oats, and whole wheat.
  • Include lean proteins such as chicken, fish, beans, and tofu.
  • Opt for healthy fats found in foods like avocados, nuts, and olive oil.
  • Avoid or limit sugary foods and processed snacks.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated throughout the day.

Understanding Nutrition

Nutrition is the study of how food affects our bodies. A balanced diet gives our body the nutrients it needs to function properly, like vitamins, minerals, protein, and energy.

There are six main types of nutrients:

  • Carbohydrates: The body's primary energy source. You can find them in foods like bread, pasta, and fruits.
  • Proteins: Help with muscle repair and immune function. Foods like eggs, beans, and chicken are rich in protein.
  • Fats: Provide long-lasting energy and are essential for cell growth. Healthy fats come from foods like nuts, seeds, and fish.
  • Vitamins: Support your immune system and overall health. Vegetables and fruits are great sources of vitamins.
  • Minerals: Help with bone health, nerve function, and overall body balance. You can find them in dairy, leafy greens, and meat.
  • Water: Vital for every cell and function in your body. Water helps with digestion, circulation, and temperature regulation.
